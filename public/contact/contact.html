Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What is the check-in and check-out time?

+

Check-in time is 2:00 PM and check-out time is 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out can be arranged based on availability.

Do you provide airport/railway station pickup?

+

Yes, we provide pickup and drop services from Calicut Airport and Kozhikode Railway Station at an additional cost. Please inform us in advance.

What activities are included in the stay?

+

Nature walks, bird watching, and guided property tours are complimentary. Adventure activities like trekking and camping can be arranged at additional charges.

Is food included in the room tariff?

+

We offer different packages - room only, with breakfast, or full board. Please check our packages page or contact us for detailed pricing.

What is your cancellation policy?

+

Free cancellation up to 7 days before check-in. 50% refund for cancellations between 3-7 days. No refund for cancellations within 3 days of check-in.
