OUR STORY

Where nature meets hospitality in the heart of Wayanad

OUR JOURNEY

A Dream Born in the Hills

Hawkins Homestay was founded in 2015 by the Hawkins family, who fell in love with Wayanad's untouched beauty. What started as a small family home has blossomed into a premier nature retreat, welcoming travelers from around the world.

We believe in sustainable tourism, authentic Kerala hospitality, and creating experiences that connect people with nature. Every corner of our homestay tells a story of passion, dedication, and love for this magnificent land.

Our Core Values

What drives us every day

Sustainability

We practice eco-friendly tourism, protecting Wayanad's natural beauty for future generations through solar energy, waste management, and organic farming.

Authentic Hospitality

Experience genuine Kerala warmth and tradition. We treat every guest as family, sharing our culture, stories, and home-cooked meals.

Community Focus

We support local artisans, farmers, and guides, ensuring tourism benefits the entire Wayanad community through fair wages and partnerships.

Adventure Spirit

From sunrise treks to wildlife safaris, we curate experiences that ignite your sense of adventure while respecting nature's boundaries.
