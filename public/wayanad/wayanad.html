DISCOVER WAYANAD

Where mist-kissed mountains meet emerald forests

The Green Paradise of Kerala

Wayanad, nestled in the Western Ghats of Kerala, is a pristine hill station that captivates visitors with its breathtaking landscapes, rich biodiversity, and tranquil atmosphere. At an altitude ranging from 700 to 2100 meters above sea level, this enchanting destination offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, wildlife, culture, and adventure.

Known for its lush green forests, sprawling tea and coffee plantations, majestic waterfalls, and exotic wildlife, Wayanad is a haven for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts. The district is home to three major wildlife sanctuaries, numerous trekking trails, ancient caves, and historical temples that speak volumes about its rich heritage.

Chembra Peak

The highest peak in Wayanad at 2,100 meters, featuring a heart-shaped lake en route. Perfect for trekking enthusiasts seeking panoramic views of the Western Ghats.

How to Reach Wayanad

Multiple convenient routes to paradise

By Air

Calicut International Airport (95 km)
Nearest airport with regular flights from major Indian cities. Taxis and buses available for onward journey.

Travel Time: 2.5 - 3 hours

By Train

Kozhikode Railway Station (90 km)
Well-connected station with trains from all major cities. Local buses and taxis readily available.

Travel Time: 2.5 - 3 hours

By Road

From Bangalore: 270 km | Mysore: 140 km
Excellent road connectivity via NH766. State-run and private buses operate regularly from neighboring states.

From Bangalore: 5-6 hours
